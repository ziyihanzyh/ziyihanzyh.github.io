About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. John C.S. Lui (ACM/IEEE fellow). Before this, I earned my Master's degree from the School of Cyber Science and Engineering (CSE) at Wuhan University (WHU), where I was mentored by Prof. Ruiting Zhou.

My previous research focused on edge intelligence (specifically optimizing model training and inference) and GPU resource sharing. My current research interests include reinforcement learning (RL), multi-armed bandits (MAB), and their applications. I welcome opportunities for collaboration and engaging discussions. Please feel free to contact me.
